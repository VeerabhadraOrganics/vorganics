How To Use Our Natural & Organic Hair Oil

For best results, follow these simple steps to deeply nourish your scalp and promote strong, healthy hair

Wash Hair With Water
Gently rinse your hair with lukewarm water to remove surface dust and open up the scalp pores for better oil absorption.
Apply Oil On Hair
Take a small amount of oil in your palm and massage it into the scalp using your fingertips. Ensure even distribution from roots to tips.
Wait And Wash
Allow the oil to penetrate deeply. For best results, leave it overnight. If you're short on time, leave it for at least 30–60 minutes.Rinse thoroughly with a gentle, natural shampoo. Avoid harsh chemicals to maintain the benefits of the oil.

How To Use Our Natural & Organic Face Pack

or best results, follow these simple steps to deeply cleanse, nourish, and refresh your skin using our herbal face packs.

Cleanse Your Face
Gently wash your face with lukewarm water and a mild cleanser to remove dirt and oil.
Mix the Face Pack
Take 1–2 teaspoons of the face pack powder in a clean bowl. Add plain water as per your skin type and mix into a smooth paste.
Apply Evenly
Apply the paste evenly over your face and neck using clean fingers or a brush. Avoid the area around the eyes and lips.Leave it on for 10–15 minutes or until it dries completely. Do not rub or scrub while it's drying.
